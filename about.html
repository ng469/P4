About Us

Goal of website:

The goal of this website is to bring awareness to people about climate change and the long term, disastrous effects of it. Public awareness regarding this phenomenon is essential to confront and tackle the extremity of climate change. It also lets people adapt to what is already an extensive global emergency. The website contains information that targets the effects of climate change on human health and society.

Target of website:

This website is targeted towards an audience that is interested in learning about immediate attention seeking global issues. It also helps people who are looking for ways to be a part of the solution and help the planet by doing their part in preventing climate change.

Topics of website:

Page topics include Importance of the study of climate change, benefits of raising awareness on this topic, some examples of the adverse effects in recent years, and preventive measures.

Call to Action of website:

After reading the content of the website, users will be directed to legitimate pages where they can sign off on petitions, if they wish, that regard climate change. They will also be able to find links to books, articles, and newsletters that discuss climate change from different perspectives.
